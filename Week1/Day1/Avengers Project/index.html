The Spider-Men

Spider-Man is arguably the most recognizable superhero in the entire world. In fact, he's such an icon, the entire Marvel multiverse revolves around the so-called "Web of Life and Destiny." Different variations of the character exist, the most popular being the friendly-neighborhood Peter Parker, but there's been a surge lately in the popularity of the Brooklyn Spider-Man, Miles Morales. Together, they protect the streets of New York while the other big-time heroes are off saving the world from galaxy-threatening menaces.
